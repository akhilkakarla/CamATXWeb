About CamATX

Experience Austin’s Traffic Like Never Before with CamATX — Live CCTV and AI-Powered Traffic Insights.

CamATX offers real-time live CCTV images from the City of Austin’s official Open Data portal, combined with powerful AI-driven traffic analytics to help you make smarter, faster travel decisions. Whether you're commuting, driving rideshare, or exploring the city, CamATX brings Austin’s roads directly to your fingertips — giving you clear, up-to-date views and insights for every journey.

Key Features

Live CCTV Feeds Across Austin:
Stream high-quality live video from traffic cameras at major intersections across the city. Monitor current road conditions, accidents, closures, or construction in real time. CamATX provides direct access to Austin’s extensive public camera network, covering the city’s traffic landscape comprehensively.

AI-Generated Congestion Pattern Analysis:
We collected 24 hours of camera images from one typical weekday and one typical weekend day, then used OpenCV to locally analyze traffic flow and congestion trends. Tap any camera marker on the interactive map under the AI-Analytics screen to view AI-generated graphs comparing typical weekday and weekend traffic patterns for that location.

Intuitive Map Interface:
CamATX’s user-friendly map shows all traffic cameras as interactive markers with live thumbnail previews. Quickly locate cameras near your route or destination. Use the address search bar at the top of the screen to quickly navigate to a specific region within Austin. Switch between live feeds and congestion graphs seamlessly.

Reliable Open Data Source:
All live CCTV images come directly from the City of Austin’s official Open Data portal, ensuring authentic, accurate, and timely feeds without delays or third-party interference.
